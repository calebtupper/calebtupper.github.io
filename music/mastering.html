Frequently Asked Questions

+−What is music mastering?

Music mastering is the process of preparing music for digital and physicial distribution, often by using analog and digital eq, compression, and other audio processing tools to increase the volume and improve the quality of the music.

+−Do I need my music mastered?

Yes, to ensure your music plays at a professionally competitive volume and clarity, you should get your music mastered.

+−What applications do you master music with?

Caleb works in Logic Pro to master music, using a combination of stock and third-party plugins to boost the volume and maximize the clarity of the music.

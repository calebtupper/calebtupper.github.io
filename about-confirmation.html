About

Tupper Music saves artists time and money by providing professional quality drum tracks faster and at a fraction of the cost of a larger studio. Drummer Caleb Tupper specializes in Blues, Rock, and Gospel genres and delivers drum parts that get your head nodding and your foot tapping.

Caleb began taking music lessons when he was six years old. In ninth grade, he started drumming and quickly felt at home behind the kit. Within a year, he joined his church worship team and played at open mic nights throughout Northern Virginia. He drummed with the Christopher Newport University Drum Line from 2014-2017, and performed internationally in front of several million at the 2016 Dublin St. Patricks Day parade.

Today, Caleb plays weekly with Catalyst Church in Newport News, Virginia. He records drums from his home studio, and mixes the tracks with Logic Pro X.
